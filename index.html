How Pillar AI Works

1
Support staff writes note using structured form (not free text). Sections for contact type, observations, outcomes, next steps.
2
AI scores quality against Pillar's standards: person-centred language, specificity, outcome recording, support plan alignment.
3
AI reframes note to improve quality while preserving factual accuracy. Gives feedback on what was wrong.
4
Support staff verifies: "This is an accurate factual depiction." AI never overwrites without human consent.
5
Scores hidden from staff. Focus on quality improvement, not metric chasing. Compliance team sees scores internally.

Known Limitations

"AI Would Rather Not" — AI refuses to reframe notes mentioning suicide, self-harm, or certain sensitive content. These notes are flagged for manual review by the compliance team. This is a known limitation, not a bug — human oversight is required for sensitive cases.
